# FUTURE_DS_03
A funnel performance dashboard or analysis report with key drop-off insights and actionable recommendation to improve conversions.

📊Project Name:

Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

💡Overview:

This project analyzes the marketing funnel to evaluate how effectively potential customers move through different stages—from awareness to conversion. The goal is to identify drop-off points, measure conversion rates, and optimize marketing strategies to improve overall business performance.

🧹 🔧Tools Used:

➤ Power BI – for creating interactive funnel dashboards

➤ Microsoft Excel – for data cleaning and preparation

➤ DAX – for calculating conversion rates and KPIs

➤ Data Modeling – to connect marketing and sales data

🧠Key Insights:

➤ Significant drop-offs observed at specific funnel stages

➤ Conversion rate decreases as users move deeper into the funnel

➤ Certain channels generate high traffic but low conversions

➤ Some campaigns perform better in converting leads into customers

➤ User behavior varies across different marketing sources

📌Recommendations:

➤ Optimize weak stages of the funnel to reduce drop-offs

➤ Focus on high-converting marketing channels

➤ Improve landing pages and user experience

➤ Use targeted campaigns for better audience engagement

➤ Track and analyze campaign performance regularly

💡Outcomes of Task 3:

✔ Built an interactive marketing funnel dashboard

✔ Identified bottlenecks in the conversion process

✔ Measured performance of different marketing channels

✔ Improved understanding of customer journey
